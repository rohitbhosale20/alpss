Privacy Policy

ALPS Softwares INC.
Last Updated: 07/12/2023

At ALPS Softwares INC., your privacy is very important to us. We know how important it is to trust the companies you work with, especially when it comes to your personal information. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and how we protect your data.

If you ever have any questions, feel free to reach out to us at privacy@alpssoftware.digital.

1. What Information We Collect

We only collect what we need to provide you with the best possible service. This includes:

  • Personal Information: Your name, email address, phone number, job title, and company name.
  • Technical Information: Things like your IP address, browser type, device details, and how you interact with our website or services.
  • Service-Related Information: Details about your interactions with us, such as service requests and support history.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and improve our IT services.
  • Communicate with you, whether to answer questions or provide support.
  • Make our services better and more secure.

We promise to only use your information for legitimate purposes and never in a way that surprises you.

3. Your Rights

We believe you should have control over your personal information. Depending on where you live, you have the following rights:

  • Access: You can ask us for a copy of the personal information we have about you.
  • Correction: If anything is incorrect or out of date, you can ask us to fix it.
  • Deletion: You can ask us to delete your information under certain conditions.
  • Limit Use: You can tell us to stop using your information in specific ways.
  • Data Portability: You can request a copy of your data in a simple format to take elsewhere.
  • Non-Discrimination (CCPA): Exercising your rights will never affect how we treat you.

To exercise any of these rights, just email us at privacy@alpssoftware.digital. We’ll get back to you as soon as we can, usually within 30 days.

4. How We Keep Your Data Safe

We take your data’s security seriously. We use modern security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access or breaches. While no system is 100% secure, we are constantly improving our practices to keep your information safe.

5. How Long We Keep Your Information

We only keep your personal data for as long as we need it to provide our services, meet legal obligations, or resolve any issues. When we no longer need your information, we will securely delete or anonymize it.

6. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. However, we may share it with:

  • Service Providers: Trusted partners who help us run our business (like payment processors or hosting providers).
  • Legal Requirements: If the law requires it, we may share your data with authorities.
  • Business Protection: To protect our company, clients, and others from harm.

7. International Data Transfers (GDPR)

If you’re outside the U.S., your information may be transferred here. We always make sure your data is protected and comply with GDPR safeguards when handling international data.

8. Children’s Privacy

We don’t knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 16. If we discover we’ve collected data from a child without consent, we’ll delete it right away.

9. Changes to Our Privacy Policy

We may update this policy occasionally to reflect changes in how we operate or legal requirements. If we make any big changes, we’ll let you know either through email or a notice on our website.
